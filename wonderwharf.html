Wonder Wharf: The Crown Jewel of Seymour's Bay

Welcome to Wonder Wharf, the historic amusement park owned by Calvin Fischoeder! This beloved landmark has been entertaining families since 1945, featuring classic rides, questionable safety standards, and unforgettable memories.

  • The Scramble Pan - Our signature spinning ride
